About SpectrumSC


SpectrumSC fosters relationships between professional LGBT networks and promotes diversity both in the workplace and on campus. Given that LGBT students are often underrepresented in business and technology careers, our goal is to connect, advise, and support these students who are considering working in business. We are a professional development society for LGBT students and allies within Marshall and USC.

SpectrumSC Case Competition

Are you a curious problem solver and a diverse undergraduate interested in business? Want the chance to win $500?

Register for the first annual SpectrumSC Diversity Case Competition! We are sponsored by EY, PwC, Wells Fargo, Bank of America Merrill Lynch, and Accenture, and parterning with USC Undergraduate Student Government, AIM Consulting, Trojan Consulting Group, Latino Business Student Association, Black Student Assembly at USC, Marshall Women's Leadership Board, USC Queers in Engineering, Science, and Technology - Quest to give diverse undergraduates an opportunity to showcase their talents! The first place team will receive $500, second place will receive $300, and third place will receive $200.

March 23rd, 6-8pm, ACC236: Learn how to prepare for a case competition and get tips from the Trojan Consulting Group
March 23rd, 11:50pm: Registration closes
March 24th: Case and teams will be released
March 29th, 11:59pm: Deliverables due
April 1st: Top 10 teams are notified of their progress to the second round
April 8th, 12-2pm: Top 10 teams present to panels of judges from EY, PwC, and Bank of America Merrill Lynch
April 8th, 3-5pm, Ground Zero: All participants are welcome to the networking reception with all sponsors. Free Mendocino Farms and Ground Zero drinks!

All participants will have their resumes included in a resume book for sponsor firms. If you progress to the second round, you wil be paired with a Marshall Faculty mentor to fine-tune your recommendation and prepare for the presentation.

No business or case experience is necessary. Register here by March 23rd, 11:59PM. You may compete as an individual or as a team or 3-4 people. If you don't have a team, we will help you find one.
