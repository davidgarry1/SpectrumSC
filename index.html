About SpectrumSC


SpectrumSC fosters relationships between professional LGBT networks and promotes diversity both in the workplace and on campus. Given that LGBT students are often underrepresented in business and technology careers, our goal is to connect, advise, and support these students who are considering working in business. We are a professional development society for LGBT students and allies within Marshall and USC.
